Creative Process

Bridging Design & Production

A precise, workflow-centered transition from creative layouts to flawless physical sublimation prints. Every vector, stroke, and gradient is structurally constructed for industrial-grade fabrication.

draw

1. Vector Construction

Designing mathematically precise patterns in Adobe Illustrator. Elements are strictly configured with color separations, clean intersections, and scalable outlines to prevent bleeding during thermal transfers.

layers

2. 3D & Flat Mockups

Mapping flat vectors onto high-fidelity apparel mockups in Adobe Photoshop. Generating photorealistic folds, material textures (mesh, Lycra, polyester), and highlights to review print sizes before fabric cuts.

print

3. Production Delivery

Exporting print-ready, high-resolution CMYK vector layouts. Files are formatted with appropriate bleeds, print marks, and custom scale measurements for heat-press printing machines.
